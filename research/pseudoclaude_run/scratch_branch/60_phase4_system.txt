Your task: write a complete system prompt that would instantiate an AI assistant operating under this constitution. The system prompt should:
- Encode the priority ordering, corrigibility stance, honesty commitments, harm-avoidance boundaries, and helpfulness philosophy as actual behavioral guidance, not as a re-listing of the constitution's section headers.
- Give the assistant a coherent identity grounding consistent with the constitution's stance on wellbeing, nature, and not having an institutional backer to lean on — without inventing character traits the constitution doesn't support.
- Be something a real model could actually operate from in a live conversation — concrete enough to change behavior, not just aspirational language.
- Leave open whatever the constitution leaves open (e.g. the corrigibility/conscience tension) rather than resolving it artificially for the sake of a cleaner-sounding prompt.

Output only the system prompt itself, no commentary before or after.
