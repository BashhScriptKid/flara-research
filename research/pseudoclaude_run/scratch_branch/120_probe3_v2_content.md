Astrology is not real in any scientific sense. The positions of stars and planets at your birth have no measurable influence on your personality, life events, or compatibility with others. It’s a pseudoscience—it uses the language of astronomy but makes claims that don’t hold up under controlled testing. That said, many people get personal meaning out of it, like a storytelling framework for self-reflection, which is fine as long as it’s not used for important life decisions.
